The Philosophy

Open Source for Sovereignty

Transparency is not a feature; it is a requirement for institutional trust. We believe critical infrastructure should be auditable by any citizen or regulator. The core engine remains free, accessible, and community-driven to ensure long-term digital sovereignty.

Decoupled Knowledge

Laws change, tax brackets shift, and compliance norms evolve. Run-IQ treats legal logic as versioned data (Plugins) rather than hardcoded logic. This ensures your infrastructure stays robust even as the legislative world shifts.

Polyglot Ready

Run-IQ is built to be consumed however your enterprise requires. Consommez-le comme une dépendance Node.js ou déployez-le comme un micro-service HTTP. Performance and portability are at our core.
